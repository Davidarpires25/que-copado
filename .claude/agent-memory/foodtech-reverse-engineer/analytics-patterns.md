# FoodTech Analytics Patterns - Detailed Notes

## Platform Comparison (Updated 2026-02)

### Fudo (fu.do) - Argentina/LATAM
- Plans: Base $30/mo, Standard $40/mo, Full $50/mo (pricing by table count)
- Reports: Sales by hour, by week, payment method pie chart, product ranking by category
- Premium (Pro): Food cost ("Costo de Mercaderia Vendida"), margin percentage view
- Export: XLS format
- Missing: No geospatial analytics, no customer CRM in basic plans, no real-time dashboard
- Integration: Rappi, UberEats, PedidosYa, DiDi Food, iFood, MercadoPago

### Toast POS (US-based)
- 2026 focus: AI "intelligence layers" on top of POS/payments data
- ML models: menu profitability in real-time, labor predictions by day/hour, anomaly detection
- Key reports: Product Mix, Sales by Hour, Sales by Day of Week, Customer Frequency, Time to Complete
- Kitchen Performance: completed ticket count + avg ticket time
- End of Day Report: standard feature

### Square for Restaurants (US-based)
- 2025 updates: Enhanced accounting reports, Square AI natural language queries
- AI-Powered Forecasts: demand, staff shifts, ingredient usage
- Live Sales Report: real-time open checks, total sales, closed checks
- Comps & Voids tracking, server performance reports
- Section sales by floor plan area

### PedidosYa Partner (LATAM)
- Limited analytics: orders, revenue, top products, Plus metrics
- Growth: 22% order growth in 2025 Argentina, restaurant orders +18.5% YoY
- 65% LATAM partners on Plus program
- No granular data access (hourly, individual customers, margins)

### iFood (Brazil)
- 2025 Retrospective for partners: total customers, order count, top 3 dishes, ratings
- 335K new restaurants in 2025 (83% increase)
- AI assistants (Cris/Karen): 296K restaurants, -45% delay cancellations
- 81% of new entrants are SMBs

## Industry-Wide Gaps (Que Copado Opportunities)
1. No POS offers geospatial heatmaps at individual business level
2. No POS breaks down shipping revenue vs free shipping impact on ticket
3. WhatsApp checkout + analytics integration is unique in LATAM
4. Daily close report via WhatsApp message = native to the workflow

## Key KPIs for Argentine Hamburgueria
- Food cost target: 28-35% of sale price
- Cancellation rate target: <5%
- Delivery time target: <30 minutes
- Customer retention: repeat rate is the #1 sustainability metric
